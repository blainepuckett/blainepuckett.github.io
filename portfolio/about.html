Hi, How Are You?

Before designing websites, emails, and other marketing collateral to sell auto parts to the masses, or prototyping and testing enterprise applications for traders and banks to sell financial securities, I was just a kid on the dial-up modem tying up the phone line and trying to make the coolest website for my high school garage band.

Back then I had no idea what I was doing. But I was learning, adapting, iterating and trying to make cool things.

Music sparked a fire in me. But it was design that kept that fire burning long after those Nirvana records became stale and my band broke up.

The ‘Do it yourself’ ethos and never being afraid to fail has taken me down some amazing paths of discovery. Sure, I may be older and wiser now but those early experiences of rolling up my sleeves, getting to work, and having mixed results still informs everything I do today.

My name is Blaine; I design solutions, I solve problems, and I love what I do.
